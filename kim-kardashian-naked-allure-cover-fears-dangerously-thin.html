KIM Kardashian posed nearly naked on the cover of Allure magazine after sparking concern with her drastic 21-lb weight loss.

In the accompanying interview, the reality star hit back at critics of her extreme dieting and insisted it was all done in a "healthy" way.

Kim wore a nude bra for the cover image of Allure's August issue, posing with her legs crossed in front of her and a huge blond braid wrapped around her body.

In other photos, the 41-year-old rocked a skintight nude mesh catsuit and a strapless black gown with gold metal detail.

While chatting to the publication, she addressed the backlash to her recent admission that she dropped 16lbs in three weeks to fit into Marilyn Monroe’s iconic dress for the Met Gala.

“If I was starving and doing it really unhealthy, I would say that, of course, that’s not a good message," the SKIMS founder said.
