Joost Luiten Wife Melanie Jane Lancaster Is Expecting Baby In December

 Posted on April 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  505 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Joost Luiten wife, Melanie Jane Lancaster, is pregnant with the firstborn of the Dutch professional golfer.

The couple shares a love of golf and supports each other, but Melanie pursued her career in golf as Joost did.

Joost Luiten, a Dutch golfer, has achieved significant success on the European Tour.

He won the NBO Oman Open in 2018, marking his sixth victory on the tour. 

Additionally, Luiten has triumphed at the KLM Open twice, becoming the first Dutch player to win his national Open twice since he joined the European Tour in 1972.

In 2013, he secured two wins and achieved his career-best 11th-place finish in the Race to Dubai Rankings.

Luiten’s golf journey began at nine when his uncle introduced him to the sport at a driving range in Rotterdam.

Joost shares his ethnicity with the rising Dutch professional football player Xavi Simons.

Joost Luiten Wife Melanie Jane Lancaster: Arrival Of New Born

Melanie Jane Lancaster, a true golf enthusiast, was born and raised in Zandvoort, where her love for the sport began.

Growing up on Nigel and Paula Lancaster’s parents’ golf course, she developed a deep connection with the game.

Melanie Jane Lancaster is a highly versatile individual with a diverse sports management, media, and entertainment background.

She is associated with the Joost Luiten Foundation, the “Melanie on Tour” blog, Golfbaan The Dunes, and GolfVrouw.nl.

In addition to her roles in golf-related events and marketing, Melanie has a history in racing, serving as a race driver instructor and competing for well-known brands.

Her multifaceted experiences reflect her adaptability and dedication in the sports and entertainment industry.

Moreover, her relationship with professional golfer Joost Luiten further marks her passion for golf.

She announced via her June 18, 2023, Instagram post that their due date for the baby is probably booked for 3 in December 2023.

Melanie Jane Lancaster Collaboration With Italian Shoe Brand

Melanie-Jane Lancaster, the partner of Joost Luiten and co-designer with Duca del Cosma, collaborated to create the MELANIE-JANE women’s golf shoe.

Likewise, catering to the demands of a busy golf, travel, and business lifestyle with hand-crafted patented leather and premium materials.

The shoe features a Waterproof Bootie System, ARNEFLEX® insole for cushioning, and Airplay VI outsole for optimal stability.

The design showcases a predominantly white shoe with sporty black trim, five stars representing high standards, and Joost’s signature orange ‘C’ for a super-premium look.

Melanie-Jane’s passion for golf and fashion shines through in this stylish and comfortable golf shoe, perfect for fashion-conscious women golfers seeking top-quality performance on and off the course.

Melanie In Almost A Life-Threatening Incident

Pro golfer Joost Luiten attempted to maintain social distancing with his caddie during a round, jokingly having him throw him a club from a distance.

However, as he tried to hit the shot, he accidentally struck his girlfriend, Melanie-Jane Lancaster, who was filming the stunt.

Fortunately, she only suffered a bruise from the incident.

Luiten humorously shared the video on social media, admitting the challenge of social distancing on the golf course.
