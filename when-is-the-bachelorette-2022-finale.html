When Is the 'Bachelorette' 2022 Finale? Season 19 Details

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  332 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

When is the 2022 finale of 'The Bachelorette'? Here's what we know about when everyone's favorite dating show will be wrapping up.

If you've been keeping up with The Bachelorette this season, odds are that you already know how heated things have gotten in the weeks of it being on the air. Season 19, which premiered on July 11, 2022, has seen Rachel Recchia and Gabby Windey set out on a journey to find love, judge a swimsuit pageant, make a pivotal decision at a rose ceremony, send a top contender home, and more.

And with this season being the first-ever in the franchise to feature two Bachelorettes for the entire season, it's clear that Season 19 is going to continue being a great ride. As always, that ride will conclude with the After the Final Rose special, but when exactly is The Bachelorette Season 19 finale? Here's what we know!

When is 'The Bachelorette' 2022 finale?

Although a lot has already transpired in the weeks that The Bachelorette has been on the air, we actually still have a ways to go until the season's conclusion. But perhaps surprisingly, that conclusion won't be on a Monday night.

Article continues below advertisement

Starting the third week of September, The Bachelorette moves to Tuesday nights with the second (yes, second!) Fantasy Suite episode on Tuesday, Sept. 13. The finale airs the following Tuesday on Sept. 20.

On top of that, ABC's Bachelor in Paradise premieres its eighth season on Tuesday, Sept. 27 at 8 p.m. ET. BiP will return for the second episode on Monday, Oct. 3. From there, the show will air two-hour episodes on both Monday and Tuesday nights for the remainder of the season.

Be sure to check out Rachel and Gabby as they continue on their unprecedented journeys to find love when The Bachelorette airs new episodes on Mondays at 8 p.m. ET on ABC, and on Tuesdays in the same timeslot beginning on Sept. 13.
