Carly Pearce dating history: Get to know the country star's ex-boyfriends

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  496 words  |  Martina Birk

CARLY Pearce is a country singer and songwriter with major hits including I Hope You’re Happy Now and Every Little Thing. 

Pearce filed for divorce from her husband, fellow country artist Michael Ray, in 2020 after eight months of marriage.

Who has Carly Pearce dated?

Michael Ray

Carly and Michael Ray tied the knot on October 6, 2019, on a Nashville-area farm before a small group of family and friends.

The two dated for a little over a year. They hit it off at a house party that Carly hosted during the summer of 2018.

The pair made their relationship red carpet official at the CMA Awards in November 2018. Ray proposed to Carly during a family vacation to Tulum, Mexico a month afterward.

The marriage lasted eight months. A source close to the couple told People magazine that the painful split was Pearce’s “last resort."

She said in an interview with Us Weekly in 2021 that "I felt like I had failed."

"I felt like … I would never find love again because I was divorced at so young, and [I] was just embarrassed," she told the magazine.

Michael Ray is also a country music singer and the two performed together at the famed Grande Ole Opry in 2018.

Riley King

Carly confirmed her relationship with former Minor League Baseball player Riley King in May 2021.

The two graced multiple red carpets and award shows together for about two years before they split in June 2023.

Riley confirmed the break up in an interview with Page Six, saying, "Carly and I have parted ways and I wish her nothing but the best in both her career and personal life."

Carly told People, "We just simply were not right for each other, and that's the bottom line."

Who is Carly Pearce?

Carly Pearce was born on April 24, 1990, in Taylor Mill, Kentucky. She is a country singer and songwriter who started performing as a teenager.

At 16, Pearce dropped out of high school and performed five nights a week at Dollywood, an amusement park in Tennessee.

The singer rose to fame with her platinum single Every Little Thing in 2017.

Pearce also boasts a handful of catchy songs, including a duet with Lee Brice called I Hope You’re Happy Now.

The song claimed the top of the Billboard Country Airplay chart.

She toured with country legends such as Blake Shelton, Rascal Flatts, and Lady Antebellum and collaborated with Luke Combs. 

Pearce has been nominated for CMT, iHeartRadio, ACM, and CMA awards, Forbes reports.

Who is Carly Pearce's rumored boyfriend?

Carly shares glimpses of her life with her fans on Instagram and she has been sharing photos and videos of her with her drummer, B.C Taylor.

The two are currently on tour together and are documenting the tour on their social media pages and Carly's fans have started speculating that the two are more than coworkers.

Neither Carly nor B.C have confirmed or denied the relationship rumors.
