Rita Ora, Charlotte Crosby, Holly Hagan and Louisa Johnson wow fans in their sexy costumes at the Ki

 Posted on September 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  531 words  |  Aldo Pusey

RITA Ora was dressed to kill when she glammed up as Poison Ivy for KISS House Party Live in London tonight.

The singer was joined by fellow performer Louisa Johnson and Geordie Shore’s finest exports Charlotte Crosby and Holly Hagan on the red carpet.

All the ladies were out to impress, with Rita wowing fans when she turned up in the cartoon baddie’s famous outfit.

Not to be outdone, X Factor star Louisa decided to turn up the heat by dressing up as Britney Spears.

Her outfit was almost a near identical match for the one worn by the US superstar in her 2001 video for I’m A Slave 4 U.

Louisa grinned for the cameras she followed Rita up the red carpet.

Both stars played up for the cameras, with Rita blowing the photographers a kiss as she arrived.

Reality stars Charlotte and Holly didn’t disappoint as the pair hit London for their second night out in a row.

Newly single Charlotte showed her ex Stephen Bear exactly what he was missing in a sexy cat-girl outfit.

The Just Tattoo Of US star oozed confidence as she walked up the red carpet and into the SSE Arena.

Charlotte's MTV co-star Holly made a lasting impression in her zombie-bride outfit.

The party loving pair were joined later on in the evening by Love Island's Amber Davies.

She appeared to have swapped some style notes with Charlotte, as she too donned a feline design.

Marcel Somerville opted to have some fun with his look, dressing up as the Joker from Batman, while his girlfriend Gabby Allen showed off a hint of Cruella De Ville, with her spotted coat and glittery make up.

Their Love Island co-stars Jessica Shears and Dom Lever caused a fright when it was their turn to pose on the red carpet.

Taking inspiration from Alice In Wonderland, Dom looked almost unrecognisable in his long red wig.

Celebs Go Dating star Nadia Essex looked sensational as Angelina Jolie’s character Maleficent and Amelia Lily went all out as Captain America.

Dua Lipa turned heads as zombie version of Little Red Riding Hood while Anne Marie, who is performing at tonight's epic bash, channelled the Wild West with her Gothic outfit.

Not everyone was in the mood for dressing up however, with some of the Towie cast putting on their finest to walk down the red carpet.

Pete Wicks was flying solo and risked an awkward run in with his ex Megan McKenna's best friend Amber Turner who was also at the event.

While James Lock and his girlfriend Yazmin Oukhellou looked every inch the loved up couple as they smiled for photos.

Nathan Massey and Cara De La Hoyde were also snapped at the event, fuelling rumours their romance was hotting up again.

They are expecting the birth of their first child later this year and looked happier than ever as they arrived at the event.
