Love is Blind: Where is the cast now? The US Sun

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1054 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

NETFLIX'S reality dating show Love is Blind quickly became one of the steamiest hit series on the streaming service.

As season two comes to a conclusion, let's take a look back on season one and see which couples are still together.

Love Is Blind couples - where are they now?

WARNING: Contains spoilers from season one of Love Is Blind.

Like Married At First Sight mixed in with Love Island, Netflix's smash-hit reality show came to a dramatic conclusion in March 2020.

The final episode of Love Is Blind saw some couples get married, while others decided to break up.

The show was filmed two years ago - so here's which couples are still going strong.

Cameron and Lauren - still together

Of course, these two are still married - they were the most solid couple throughout the series.

In December 2019, both Cam and Lauren posted vacation photos from Cancun, though they didn't tag each other or post photos of one another - this could be because the show hadn't aired yet.

Lauren also revealed in an interview with Elite Daily that they are still together.

"Oh my God. We're so happy," she gushed. "It's just, like, every day we learn more and more about each other, and we honestly fall more and more in love with each other."

Cameron posted a photo from their wedding day, simply captioning the pic, "The happiest day of my life."

Then in the reunion show, they proved they were still going strong.

They recently celebrated their second wedding anniversary on November 16, 2020, and are still going strong.

Lauren shared an adorable tribute on Instagram: "Wow 2 whoooole years! We’ve made it through one of the craziest years of our lives!

"We came out stronger than ever."

The reality TV star also said she's looking forward to 80 more to come.

Lauren and Cameron recently just celebrated their third wedding anniversary and are still going strong as of March 2022. They also co-wrote a book together.

Barnett and Amber - still together

Despite Barnett having cold feet on the morning of the wedding, they still got hitched.

And we are pleased to reveal they are still married.

In the reunion show, they revealed they are still very much together.

Amber has reportedly changed her last name to Barnett.

Barnett even marked their second wedding anniversary with a cuddly snap on Instagram.

He added the hashtags #loveofmylife and #anniversary and wrote, "Here's to 2 years and a million more years together."

Amber added her own steamy beach photo to mark the milestone on IG.

She wrote: "Two years ago today since we officially said 'I do'... and I still can’t keep my hands off you! Happy 2yr Wedding Anniversary to my frustrating-crazy-sweet-handsome hubby. I LOVE YOU."

The two look inseparable and post tons of snaps from their adventures on their Instagrams.

"I wouldn't want to be soaking in the rays with anyone else, anywhere else," Barnett captioned a recent pic.

Damian and Giannina - not together

Damian and Giannina were on and off again, however they officially called in quits in 2021.

Giannina is now dating Blake Hortsmann, who was on Becca Kufrin's season of The Bachelorette.

Giannina and Blake met while filming a show together for Paramount+

Mark and Jessica - not together

Well, that was obvious.

Since the vacation in Mexico, Jessica grew more and more distant from Mark.

The poor guy had to deal with being second place; as we know, she really wanted Barnett to propose to her.

So it wasn't a surprise she didn't go through with the wedding.

Mark has since revealed to the New York Post that he had not spoken to Jessica since that day, though he had reached out at one point.

"I didn't hear back, but to me, it's just sending love out there," he said, adding that doing Love Is Blind was still the "best" thing to happen to him. "Without Jessica, there would not be a Mark of today."

On the Fourth of July, Jessica and her new boyfriend, Benjamin McGrath, MD, celebrated their first anniversary.

She captioned an Instagram post: "One year and I'm still reeling ♥️."

On July 6, 2021, Mark announced he was dating a new girlfriend, Aubrey Rainey.

The two just celebrated their first anniversary together, and they welcomed a son, Ace, back in April 2021.

Kenny and Kelly - not together

Viewers were surprised when Kelly revealed she didn't feel a real physical attraction for Kenny and chose not to go through with the marriage, much to her family's surprise.

It came as a shock as she was initially smitten with him.

Since their wedding day, Kenny has moved to Charlotte, North Carolina, while Kelly still lives in Atlanta, working as a health and empowerment coach.

One of her recent Instagram posts was about being single in your 30s and said: "I know boundaries now. I love myself so much that I won't just be with a guy bc I'm lonely."

Kenny recently announced his engagement on Instagram to someone new.

He captioned the photo, "Fiancé has a nice ring to it...Alexandra and I are excited to announce our engagement! To all of our friends, family and acquaintances, thank you for the continued love and support - we appreciate everyone and cannot wait to celebrate this next step together."

Kelly was among the commenters wishing Kenny and his new fiancé congratulations.

"OH MY GOSHH YAYYY! That's so exciting!!! I'm so excited for you two!!" Kelly wrote.

"My mouth dropped but in the best way!!!!! You deserve all the happiness Kenny!!!! Sending you both love and light! Hope to meet her someday!"

Carlton and Diamond - not together

These guys were done just a few weeks into the show when they were still on vacation in Mexico.

While they were one of the six couples to get engaged in the pods, Diamond and Carlton didn't make it to the end of their trip.

They had an explosive fight after Carlton revealed to her that he was bisexual.

The two are currently not in touch, as Carlton is happily dating his new boyfriend.

Carlton has posted a few pics on his Instagram: "Trust the MAGIC of new beginnings," he wrote under a recent post.

Meanwhile, Diamond is currently single.
