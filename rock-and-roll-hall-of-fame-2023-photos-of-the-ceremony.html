The ‘2023 Rock & Roll Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony’ special includes Carrie Underwood, Olivia Rodrigo, and more. See all the incredible photos.

The greatest artists in music turned out for the 2023 Rock & Roll Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony. ABC is airing the ceremony in a 3-hour special on January 1, 2024. The night is filled with performances from Carrie Underwood, Olivia Rodrigo, Chris Stapleton, Adam Levine, Sia, and more.

The Rock & Roll Hall of Fame Induction Ceremony honored this year’s inductees: Kate Bush, Sheryl Crow, Missy Elliott, George Michael, Willie Nelson, Rage Against the Machine and The Spinners, along with DJ Kool Herc and Link Wray for Musical Influence, and Chaka Khan, Al Kooper, Bernie Taupin for Musical Excellence, as well as Don Cornelius for the Ahmet Ertegun Award.
