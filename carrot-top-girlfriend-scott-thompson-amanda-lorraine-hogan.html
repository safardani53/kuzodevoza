Who is Carrot Top?

Scott Thompson, widely known as Carrot Top, is an American comedian born on February 25, 1965, in Rockledge, Florida.

The comedian got his name while he was in high school where his swimming coach gave him his now-famous name.

Carrot Top booked his first stand-up comedy gig as a freshman at Florida Atlantic University.

In 1992, he got his first big gig when he replaced comedian, Sam Kinison after he tragically passed away in a car accident.

From 1995 to 1999, Carrot Top worked as a continuity announcer for Cartoon Network.

In the early 2000s, he landed TV roles in Larry the Cable Guy's Christmas Spectacular, George Lopez, and Scrubs.

His comedy routines have been deemed unique due to his usage of props throughout his skits.

Due to his uncommon approach to comedy, he has been deemed the "King of Props."

Who is Carrot Top’s girlfriend Amanda Hogan?

Carrot Top has been dating his longtime girlfriend Amanda Hogan since 2014.

Amanda Hogan is a business owner born in August 1983.

Hogan is the founder of a catering and event-planning company called Any Thyme Catering.

Additionally, Hogan has a pottery and handmade tableware shop via Etsy.

Though Hogan and Carrot Top keep their relationship private, the two most likely met in Las Vegas as her company is currently based there.

What is Carrot Top’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Carrot Top has a net worth of $70million.

Apart from Carrot Top's TV and stand-up comedy appearances, the comedian has had a residency comedy show at the Luxor Hotel in Las Vegas since 2005.

In 2019, he signed a contract extension granting him residency through 2025.

His residency includes meet-and-greet packages where fans can interact with the comedian before his show.
