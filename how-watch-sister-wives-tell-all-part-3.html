What is Sister Wives?

Sister Wives is a reality television program that follows polygamist Kody Brown and his family - full of 18 children and four wives: Meri, Janelle, Christine, and Robyn.

With the series first premiering on September 26, 2010; the Brown family moved from Utah to Las Vegas, followed by Arizona.

Kody is only legally married to Robyn, and refers to his relationships with the other wives as "spiritual unions."

Meri and Kody were also legally married at one point, having divorced in 2014.

Will there be a Sister Wives season 17?

Season 17 of Sister Wives is reportedly in the works.

According to one of Christine's sons, Paedon, his mother has continued filming the show despite announcing she and Kody have split.

The series is rumored to return in late 2022 or early 2023.
