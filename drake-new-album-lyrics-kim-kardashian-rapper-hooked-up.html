DRAKE has included a reference to Kim Kardashian on his latest album amid rumors that the duo hooked up some time ago.

Fans are dissecting the lyrics online, with some slamming the mention of the Hulu star as "corny."

Drake, 36, release his new album Her Loss this week, and fans have picked up on a nod to Kim, 42 in one of his songs.

The rapper teamed up with 21 Savage for a song called More M's.

In the song, 21 Savage raps: "Put her SKIMS on, now she actin' like she Kim."

Fans shared the lyrics on a Kardashian-themed Reddit page, along with their reactions.

"Kim mentions all over this album," another person noted.

Neither the SKKN mogul nor her ex Kanye West has responded to the lyric.

Kanye did, however, have something to say about another lyric on Drake's album which appeared to reference him.

On Circo Loco, yet another son with 21 Savage, the rapper said: "Linkin with the ops, b***h, I did that for J Prince."

The lyric appears to reference a concert the pair were supposed to do together for Larry, the former leader of Chicago's Gangster Disciples who is currently serving multiple life sentences.

Drake did end up performing at the show, which fans thought marked the end of an ongoing feud between them, but Kanye took up the majority of stage time.

Kanye hit back at the lyric on Twitter, writing: "Enough already. I done gave this man his flowers multiple times. Let's really see who are real ops in this music game. Imagine all the rappers on the same side and everyone cleaning up each other's contracts. It's kingdom time. Love Drake."

Their feud stems back several years and involves a lot of moving parts.

In 2018, Drake accused Kanye of being the source that leaked the news that he secretly fathered a child to Pusha T, who made it public on The Story of Adidon.

Kanye went on to post Drake's address on Instagram, deleting it shortly after.

The Canadian rapper hit back with a diss on his Certified Lover Boy album on a song called 7am on Bridle Path.

Rumors that Drake and Kim hooked up did not help matters any.

COURTING KIM

Allegations of intimacy between the former Degrassi star and the reality star have been swirling for some time.

Drake reignited the buzz in 2021 when sources claimed he was ready to make a go at the mom of four following her split from Kanye.

A source told Heat magazine: "Drake could never understand what Kim saw in Kanye - he was saying for years how he wasn't the right guy for Kim and it would all end in tears.

"He was surprised it took her so long to realize, and started messaging her the moment it became clear that the divorce was inevitable.

"He's ready to see her whenever she says the word, basically, and he's confident that will happen fairly soon."

Kim officially filed for divorce from Kanye in February 2021 after months of speculation that their seven-year marriage was over.

Around the same time those reports surfaced, Drake's fans speculated that he dropped a hint about sleeping with Kim behind Kanye's back.

In the song Wants and Needs, he rapped: "Yeah, I probably go link to Yeezy I need me some Jesus. But as soon as I start confessin’ my sins, he wouldn’t believe us."

It comes after the award-winning artist sparked rumors he slept with Kim back in 2018.

Drake then appeared to insinuate he'd slept with Kim on his track In My Feelings.

On the record, he rapped about having sex with a woman by the name of Kiki, which is Kim's family nickname.
