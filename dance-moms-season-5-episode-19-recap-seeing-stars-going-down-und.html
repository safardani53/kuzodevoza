Seeing Stars, Going Down (Under)

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  350 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 19 brought the gang back together to rehash the drama of the past 18 weeks and stir up some more for good measure.

What did we learn from this painful reunion installment on Lifetime?

Watch Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 19 Online

Well, for starters, on Dance Moms Season 5 Episode 19 we learned that the Abby Lee Miller-driven reality series is officially going down … under.

Oh yeah baby. If you watch Dance Moms online, you know the ALDC is not opposed to traveling great distances for championships and exposure.

Australia, which is where they’re off to next, takes the cake.

Abby and her charges are off to Sydney for a two episode special, “Dance Moms Down Under, Part 1" and "Part 2," to air May 19 and May 26.

“I take my team a lot of places… I have master classes, I have a showcase where the girls will perform,” Abby dished of her plans for the trip.

While the moms confirmed that most of the ALDC dancers, including newcomer JoJo Siwa, will be in attendance, there will be some exceptions.

Holly Frazier revealed that daughter Nia has been left out of some of Abby’s excursions, and vowed to do what it takes to showcase her talent.

“She is part of ALDC, but she is excluded,” Holly lamented.

“If Abby does not want to embrace the talents of Nia as a member of her team … I will make my own opportunities for Nia as I did in L.A.”

Nia, who recently released her single "Star in Your Own Life," can be seen performing in Australia with just one teammate, JoJo, by her side.

Also teased is a pretty epic argument between dance moms Melissa Gisoni and Kira Girard, as well as some intense back-stage moments.

Standard Dance Moms fare, really. Just Australia style.
