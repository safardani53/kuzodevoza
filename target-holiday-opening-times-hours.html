Target Christmas 2023: What are the opening and closing times?

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  390 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

OVER the last few years, many retailers have chosen to keep their doors closed on major holidays.

With the holidays right around the corner, consumers now want to know if Target will be open on Christmas Eve and Christmas Day.

Is Target open on Christmas?

Target will be joining thousands of retailers nationwide and closing their doors on Christmas Day.

However, the retail giant did announce that it would be extending its hours on Christmas Eve, allowing customers to make last-minute purchases.

Target will remain open from 7 am to midnight on December 23, 2023.

Target will also remain open from 7 am to 8 pm on December 24, 2023.

While all stores will be closed on Christmas Day, they'll reopen at their regular times on December 26, 2023.

Customers are highly encouraged to check their local store hours before making a trip to avoid any store closures.

What holidays does Target observe?

In addition to being closed on Christmas Day, Target is also closed on Easter Sunday and Thanksgiving.

In 2021, Target CEO Brian Cornell said that the Thanksgiving closure would become the new tradition for years to come.

“We...closed our stores on Thanksgiving Day (in 2020) in order to minimize crowds and help our guests take the stress out of getting the best deals of the season," the company said.

"The response was so positive that we’ll carry it forward this year, keeping our Target stores closed all day long on Thanksgiving Day."

However, Target remains open for other federal holidays, including Juneteenth, Independence Day, Memorial Day, and Labor Day, among others.

Opening and closing times may vary on those days, so it is always recommended to check with local stores for exact hours of operation.
