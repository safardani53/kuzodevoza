Brief Glance on J.J Barea

J.J. Barea (fully named José Juan Barea Mora) is a professional basketball player currently playing for the Cangrejeros de Santurce of the Baloncesto Superior Nacional (BSN). Before this, he has also played for the NBA Development League and the Baloncesto Superior Nacional.

Initially, Barea first played basketball for the Indios de Mayagüez of the Baloncesto Superior Nacional (BSN). During his collegiate years at Northeastern University, Barea averaged 2.8 points in 14 games.

By the time J.J. Barea was leaving the university, he had stood as the second all-time leader in assists and three-point field goals. Well, after college, he played for the Portsmouth Invitational Tournament in Virginia. 

Furthermore, as for his professional career, J.J. Barea has played for Cangrejeros de Santurce, Dallas Mavericks, Minnesota Timberwolves, and Movistar Estudiantes. At present, Barea is the head coach of Indios de Mayagüez of the BSN.
