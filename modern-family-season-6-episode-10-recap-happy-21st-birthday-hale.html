Modern Family Season 6 Episode 10 Recap: Happy 21st Birthday, Haley!

 Posted on May 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  294 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Modern Family Season 6 Episode 10 saw Haley turn 21, her parents take her out to a bar to celebrate, and Mitch and Cam realize they’re not as cool as they thought.

Watch Modern Family Season 6 Episode 10 Online

Haley was the big star of Modern Family Season 6 Episode 10, or at least her big day was. In reality it felt like excited mom Claire was the one turning 21.

Claire said she would treat Haley like an adult more now that she’s 21, but awkwardly for Haley, this apparently includes in-depth talks about men and sex.

Cam and Mitchell believed that they got the perfect gift for Haley, but after she responded in a high-pitched tone, they were certain she hated their effort.

The two dads also thought a bachelorette party at the bar wanted to hang out with them, but when they came up to them to dance, the women blew them off.

 

Later, Claire and Haley bonded more and the latter revealed that she wants to get a tattoo. Gloria is all for it and Claire has to try to act like she supports it.

Haley suggests mother and daughter tattoos and Claire, touched that she’s included in such a thing, can’t bring herself to back out and really gets one.

Naturally, Haley thought better of it and got cold feet.

While this was going on, Phil and Jay went to buy Haley a car, and obviously argued over who could get the best deal, while Lily asked where babies come from.

She asked Manny, Luke, and Alex. It was classic.
