Who is Amanda Batula's husband Kyle Cooke?

Kyle Cooke was born on August 4, 1992, in Baltimore, Maryland.

In addition to his work on reality TV, he is an actor who, according to his IMDb page, appeared in 2013's Across Dystopia and 2023's The Traitors.

According to his LinkedIn profile, Cooke is also a businessman with a "successful track record in sales (B2C, B2B, B2B2C), business development, brand strategy, revenue growth, and product development."

As his LinkedIn also states, he is the founder of FENIX, an "invite-only app and private wellness community."

When did Amanda Batula and Kyle Cooke get married?

Amanda Batula and Cooke tied the knot on Saturday, September 25, 2021.

The wedding took place in the backyard of Batula's parents' home in Hillsborough, New Jersey.

As Bravo discussed, there was some drama involving a wedding contract and Cooke's drinking that aired on Summer House.

However, the pair were able to work through their issues and move forward with their relationship.

As discussed by PEOPLE, Batula and Cooke had to push back their initial September 2020 wedding plans due to the coronavirus pandemic.

As PEOPLE also reported, the couple got engaged on September 24, 2018 after dating for two years. They met in 2015.

Following the engagement, Batula told the publication: "It’s surreal. Aside from how funny and sexy he is, Kyle’s my best friend. He’s the person I go to for everything. And he forces me to be a better person in different ways by pushing me outside of my comfort zone.

"Life with him is exciting. And to think he wants to spend the rest our his life with me and I feel the same way, it’s just a real happy feeling.”

Cooke said: "Amanda’s the most authentic, genuine person I’ve ever met. I really look up to her. She’s incredibly selfless, thoughtful, generous and accommodating.

"She puts family and friends as the top priority, and bends over backwards for the people that are important to her. That’s what you look for in someone you want to spend the rest of your life with. She’s a hell of a partner."
