KHLOE Kardashian has flaunted the amazing landscaping that surrounds her huge mansion.

The 39-year-old shared several photos of her garden on her Snapchat account, showing off her collection of colorful flowers like asters and achilleas.

Khloe started by posting a photo of the vines crawling up her home that feature white flowers and the bushes with pink flowers in front of the vines.

She also shared a photo of the same area from a different point of view that shows the gray brick pathway around the edge of the garden.

The Good American founder posted two photos of trees in her yard, one with bright pink flowers and one with purple.

After, she posted photos of drought-tolerant perrenial flowers like yellow achilleas and purple asters.

The Kardashians star also showed off her three gray rustic pots of various sizes that sit on her front stoop and the hand-painted stones around her walkway.

Her marvelous garden is located on her $17million property in Los Angeles.

SURROUNDING THE GARDEN

The mom of two has shared lots of pics and videos of her huge California home.

In recent photos, she showed off her patio, with the large glass doors opened halfway, and the sunrise was peaking over the mountains that gave a morning glow to her backyard.

On her terrace, she had two chairs and tables for people to enjoy the serene scene.

She's also shown more photos of her stunning flower garden on her social media.

In one shot, her backyard showed pink garden roses that were surrounded by blue and white delphiniums.

Another artsy shot showed a climber with small white buds called solanum - commonly referred to as the potato vine.

Khloe showed off various colorful flowers that she has been growing in pots, as well as some herbs, like thyme.

The reality star currently has pink and peach-colored rose bushes decorating her mansion's landscape.

MILLION DOLLAR HOME

Earlier this month, the Hulu star shared more photos of her backyard oasis as she captured a ton of fish swimming in a pond and an arch over a walkway into the yard.

She gave another close-up view of her rose garden beside her massive in-ground swimming pool.

Earlier this spring, the star shared a video of her daughter True, 5, as she played on a trampoline in the backyard.

True smiled happily and blew a kiss at her mother as she bounced on the massive trampoline, while birds and a dog could be heard in the background.

Following this, Khloe shared a golden hour shot of a display in the garden, with large mushroom shapes built out of stone, surrounded by pretty wildflowers across the grass.

After a lengthy building process, the TV personality moved into the home in November 2021.
