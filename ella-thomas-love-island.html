Who is Ella Thomas from Love Island 2023?

 Posted on February 15, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  491 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

LOVE Island has upped the ante for their 2023 summer series.

Supermodel Ella Thomas is part of the intake into the legendary villa.

Who is Ella Thomas?

Ella Thomas is an award-winning model from Glasgow, Scotland.

A Love Island source told The Sun: "Ella is breathtaking to look at - it’s not hard to see why she’s a top model in her field.

“Bosses have swayed towards more regular girl and boy next door hotties in recent years but casting Ella marks a step change.

“Even better, though, she has the chat to match.

“Bosses are confident Scottish firecracker Ella is going to make a splash this series.”

The boys of the villa have their work cut out for them it seems.

Before heading into the villa, Ella told ITV: "I'm the whole package, I know what I want in life and I’ve got a big heart.

"I’d let the boys know that I’m wifey material so don’t play with me!"

Ella has appeared in big projects also, she told ITV: "I’ve been in a Headie One and Burna Burna Boy music video and I was once an extra in World War Z.

"I was 12 years old when I filmed it and got to meet Brad Pitt which was cool."

The supermodel was named Female Model of the Year at the Scottish media awards in October 2022.

Is Ella on Instagram?

The Scottish beauty does have Instagram.

She posts regularly, sharing her looks, her fits and her glamorous lifestyle.

From her aesthetic to her style, you can see why she is at the top of her game.

Who is in the Love Island 2023 cast?

The cast of the Summer edition of Love Island 2023 brings with it a new batch of hunks in trunks and bikini babes.

The Islanders joining Ella in the villa this summer so far is:

  • Andre Furtado, business owner
  • Mitchel Taylor, gas engineer
  • Catherine Agbaje, real estate agent
  • George Fensom, business development executive
  • Molly Marsh, actress and content creator
  • Ruchee Gurung, beautician
  • Ella Thomas, supermodel
  • Jess Harding, beauty clinic owner
  • Tyrique Hyde, semi-professional footballer
  • Mehdi Edno, French model

Who won Love Island 2022?

Davide Sanclimenti and Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu's time in the villa was anything but a smooth ride, but they managed to win the public's hearts - and the ITV2 reality show.

The pair were crowned champs and took home the £50,000.

Ekin said at the time: "It feels so surreal finding the man of your dreams and just growing."

For the winter 2023 season Kai Fagan and Sanam Harrinanan were crowned the winners.

When does Love Island start?

Love Island kicks off on June 5, 2023, at 9pm on ITV2 and ITVX.
