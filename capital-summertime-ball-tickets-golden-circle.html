THE Capital Summertime Ball is presumably one of the most exciting concerts of the summer, with the world's hottest artists performing their biggest hits.

Over the years, the line-up has never failed to impress ticket holders, and it seems that this year’s edition is no different. Find out who’ll be performing below...

When do tickets for the Summertime Ball 2019 go on sale?

The Summertime Ball is back - and it's now time to grab your ticket.

If you weren't one of the lucky Vodafone customers who bagged pre-sale tickets then your chance is now.

Tickets for the Summertime Ball 2019 will go on general sale at 8am (Thursday, May 2) and can be bought from the official Capital Summertime Ball website.

You can find out the line-up which includes Rita Ora and The Jonas Brothers here.

What are the price of the tickets? How much does the Golden Circle cost?

Tickets range from £49.99 to £350.

Golden Circle tickets, which are the best in the stadium, range from £126.50 to £181.50.

All prices will include a booking fee.

More music tours and festivals

When is the Summertime Ball 2019?

The Summertime Ball will take place on Saturday June 8, 2018.

The one-day festival will be held by Global at Wembley Stadium in London.

The event is promoted and broadcast by radio station Capital 95-106 FM.
