JOEY Essex is no stranger to Celebs Go Dating having tried THREE times to find love at the agency.

But this week he caused chaos without even being on the show as he "turned Lottie Moss's head".

The flirty pair enjoyed late-night drinks before jumping into a rickshaw.

They were pictured giggling as they took a series of selfies from the vehicle, which had neon pink seats.

Kate Moss's sister Lottie has previously been linked to Brooklyn Beckham and Roman Kemp.

Before that she left Vamps drummer Tristan Evans and called off a whirlwind romance with rapper Milkavelli.
