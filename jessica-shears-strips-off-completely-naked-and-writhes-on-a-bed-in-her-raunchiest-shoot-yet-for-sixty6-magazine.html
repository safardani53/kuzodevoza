JESSICA Shears has stripped down for her raunchiest shoot yet as she poses up a storm in the latest issue of SIXTY6 magazine.

The Love Island star has been more than happy to show off her enviable curves ever since taking part in the hit reality show this summer.

And Jess is making sure that fans will get their fill in the magazine shoot which sees her lying completely naked on top of a bed.

The 24-year-old beauty proceeds to showcase her incredible figure by pulling a variety of poses whilst naked in the bedroom chosen for the shoot.

She actually covers up in some of the snaps as she sexily drapes a towel around her body and pulls an innocent face at the camera.

In other snaps she shows off her cleavage in a plunging beige bodysuit, as well as a striking red jumpsuit.

The glamour model has appeared in a number of pictures for the magazine, happily posing completely naked.

Despite looking amazing, Jessica has been the victim of cruel trolls - leaving her with no choice but to tell fans she was getting her boobs redone.

She tweeted: "lol at people going in on my boobs - i'm the first one to criticise my boob job -  that's why i'm getting them re-done 😂 idiots."

She is currently very much in demand following her stint on the hit TV show Love Island, which ended last month.

MORE FROM LOVE ISLAND

Jess found love with fellow islander Dom, after stealing him away from Montana Brown in a shock twist during the first episode of the new series.

They’ve proved critics wrong by making their romance work on the outside after they were both dumped in separate eliminations.

The smitten duo even got matching Love Island palm tree tattoos done last week to mark their time on the show.

However, when Love Island host Caroline Flack pointed out on the recent reunion show that it was a permanent and serious commitment to each other, they jokingly replied it was actually more of a commitment to Love Island.

They have, however, hinted that wedding bells could happen down the line.

In an exclusive interview with The Sun last month, Dom, 26, said the couple were "having a good run" and added: "If it carries on like that outside the villa then I do see myself proposing.

“I fell in love with her. It was so difficult for me to admit that to Jess.

"I cried when she left the villa."

The loved-up couple also revealed exclusively to The Sun's Bizarre column that they've already met each other's parents and been given the seal of approval by both families.

Jessica said: "My mum and dad adore him already so he’s an easy one. They approve.

“I’ve met his mum. It was quick as we were in a rush and we didn’t have time for the whole family to be there. At some point hopefully we will go for a meal.

“I’m looking forward to taking him to a little village in Devon where no one knows who we are.”
