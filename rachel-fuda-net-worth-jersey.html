Get to know Rachel Fuda from RHONJ

 Posted on April 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Aldo Pusey

VIEWERS of the Real Housewives of New Jersey will notice some fresh faces in season 13 of the show.

One of the new cast members is named Rachel Fuda, and now fans want to learn more about her.

Who is Rachel Fuda?

Rachel Fuda was born on May 1, 1991.

She is originally from Franklin Lakes, New Jersey.

Rachel has two young girls, Gianella Jolie, and Giuliana Rose Fuda, and is also a stepmother to her teenage stepson Jaiden Fuda.

Rachel works full-time, and her tagline for her debut season is: "I'm too busy running a business to care about yours."

She was brought onto the show after becoming close with Melissa Gorga.

What is Rachel Fuda's net worth?

Rachel Fuda's net worth is currently unknown.

We know that Bravo's RHOBH housewives make between $36k and $1million per season.

Rachel has two degrees and helps run her husband's bustling businesses: Valet King, which is a luxury valet company, as well as Fuda Tile, which is a family-owned company started by her husband's father.

She also helps with a restaurant owned by her husband.

Is Rachel Fuda married?

While working as a bartender at the 46 Lounge in Totowa, New Jersey, Rachel met her husband, John Fuda.

Her husband was already a father of one, a teenager named Jaiden.

After they married and had two daughters together, Rachel decided it was time to formally adopt Jaiden - a large part of her storyline in her first season as a housewife.

She told Distractify: "Everyone will get to see the adoption process and how that comes to fruition.

“And, you know, how that gradually takes place.

"So I'm really excited to connect with other blended families and people that are, you know, maybe going through similar situations, and, you know, connecting and talking through that and hearing from people. You know, just like relating.”
