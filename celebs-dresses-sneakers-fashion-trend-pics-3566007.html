See The Fashion Trend Hollywood Life

 Posted on February 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  427 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Spring has officially sprung! Now that the temperatures are finally warming up, stars are stepping out in their best threads and favorite trends. One such style choice is sneakers paired with a favorite, cute dress. It’s a fun, casual, and simple look perfect for a day on the town. Effortlessly feminine and flirty, so many celebrities have worked the look in the past. Check out some of the stars who love to rock sneakers with dresses!

Hailey Baldwin

Hailey Baldwin is a huge fan of this trend and the model is always mixing and matching fancy pieces with casual footwear. She tried this trend and the polka dot trend at once when she was out in LA on September 25, 2019 rocking a spaghetti strap Reformation Addison Sleeveless Dress with a pair of Alexander Wang Stadium Sneakers. She accessorized with a Prada Logo-Plaque Nylon Shoulder Bag, black Celine Cl41468 Edge Sunglasses, and Jennifer Fisher Samira Hoop Earrings.

Emily Ratajkowski

Emily Ratajkowski was a huge fan of this trend in 2019. The model was spotted taking her pup, Colombo, out for a walk around New York City on August 2, for which she threw on a skintight yellow mini dress from her very own brand, Inamorata. The bodycon dress featured thick straps and a low-cut square neckline that showed off ample cleavage, while the hem of the dress was super short, revealing her long, toned and tanned legs. She styled the super tiny frock with a pair of gray Nike Air Vapormax ’97 Sneakers, skinny cat-eye Linda 965 C2 Sunglasses, a Hayward Mini 1712 Basket purse in Black Python, and a Jennifer Meyer Good Luck Charm Necklace.

Rihanna

Rihanna has never been one to shy away from tyring unique trends. On a balmy New York City day, the singer and fashion mogul stepped out in a pink satin dress with a jean jacket and Converse sneakers. The Savage X Fenty founder tossed her ‘do atop her head and sported a few bangles and necklaces to accessorize her look!

Kendall Jenner

Kendall Jenner had a perfect cute combo of mini-dress and sneakers when she went to a meeting at the Andaz Hotel in Los Angeles. She wore a chic, knit gray mini-dress with long sleeves and a pair of white sneakers for her ensemble. Kendall looked super cute in the casual look, which she put together quite effortlessly for a busy day out and about in Los Angeles!
