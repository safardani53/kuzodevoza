Boohoo is selling matching dog and human Christmas jumpers priced from 10

IF YOU don't want to leave your dog out of your festive frolics Boohoo might just have come to the rescue.

The online fashion brand has launched matching pooch and owner Christmas jumpers and prices start at just £10.

  • Reindeer Fairisle Christmas Jumper in Red, Boohoo, £15, buy now

At only £15 for the 'human jumpers', and £10 for the doggy version, there's no excuse not to invest in some matching threads for you and your pet.

The jumpers also come in black, navy blue, bottle green, a bright red and a deep burgundy shade.

However, the matching knitwear is selling out fast, so you best head over to Boohoo if you fancy snapping them up.
