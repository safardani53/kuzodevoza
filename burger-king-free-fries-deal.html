Burger King 'extends' fan favorite offer until the end of 2023 - but not everyone will grab the deal

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  320 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

BURGER King has extended one of its most popular deals until the end of 2023.

To access this offer, you’ll need to be a member of Royal Perks. 

Burger King is extending its free fries with any purchase offer until the end of 2023.

The deal is only available to Royal Perks members, although the rewards program is totally free to join.

To get your free fries of any size, you’ll need to order via the app.

Add the offer to your cart before placing an order.

You can enjoy the complimentary menu item with any purchase excluding mix and match, mix and match meals, and the Your Way meal.

The free fries offer can only be used once per week.

It comes after Burger King and a number of fast food competitors offered fans free fries without any purchase required last week.

Stores like McDonald’s, Wendy’s, and White Castle handed out free food to celebrate National French Fry Day on Friday, July 14.

Meanwhile, Arby’s has introduced a new menu featuring its most popular items for just $1, $2, or $3.

Here’s what you can get for three bucks or less:

  • $3 Classic French dip and Swiss
  • $2 Classic roast beef
  • $2 Jomacha shake
  • $2 Mozzarella Sticks
  • $1 Curly fries 

These limited-time prices will be available through July 30 or while supplies last.
