A RIDE-ON mower is the garden luxury that we would all love - and the good news is you no longer need to spend a fortune to get one.

Petrol-powered ride-on mowers will eat up the largest of lawns in quick time, either storing the waste grass in a basket, or expelling it to the side for collection.

If your garden is big enough to warrant a ride-on mower, but you don't want to break the bank, there are budget-friendly options out there.

A ride-on mower is an investment as most expensive models can set you back more than £4000.

But some decent models are selling for under £2,000 and we've rounded them up here.

Engine size, cutting circle and grass storage capacity are all important factors to consider when choosing your ride-on mower and it all depends on what kind of garden you have.

We’ve looked at user reviews and expert insights to find the best value ride-on mowers on the market today.
