TRAMAR Dillard is a popular rapper who rose to fame under the stage name Flo Rida.

Since entering the spotlight in 2007, the GRAMMY Award nominee has been linked to several women.

Who are Flo Rida's ex-girlfriends?

While Flo Rida might be a popular rapper, he often keeps details of his personal life out of the media.

However, that does not mean that he has not been spotted out in public with women before.

His dating history includes:

Phoenix White

Phoenix White is a singer who often keeps her personal life out of the spotlight.

She dated Flo Rida briefly from 2007 to 2008, according to multiple online reports.

Brenda Song

Brenda Song rose to fame on Disney Channel in the early 2000s as the rich hotel heiress London Tipton on The Suite Life of Zack & Cody and its spinoff, The Suite Life on Deck.

Brenda and Flo Rida dated briefly in 2009, according to multiple online reports.

After their split, she had an on-and-off relationship from 2010 to 2017 with Miley Cyrus' half-brother Trace Cyrus.

On January 26, 2022, it was confirmed Brenda was engaged to Home Alone star Macaulay Culkin.

The couple has two children together; a son named Dakota Song Culkin and a second baby whose name has not been disclosed yet.

Meanwhile, Flo Rida moved on with GRAMMY Award winner Brandy Norwood.

Brandy Norwood

Brandy Rayana Norwood, better known by the stage name Brandy, is a singer, songwriter, record producer, actress, and model.

Brandy and Flo Rida appeared to casually date from 2009 to 2010.

The singer confessed to VIBE: "I don’t want to say that we’re a couple.

"I can definitely say that he’s somebody in my life and I’m definitely interested but I’m not serious with anybody.

"I’m a little bit afraid to give my heart away right now. It’s very scary.”

Brandy has one daughter named Sy'rai Iman Smith from her past relationship with Robert 'Big Bert' Smith.

Eva Marcille

Eva Marcille is an actress, fashion model, and television personality who rose to fame after winning America's Next Top Model in 2004.

The pair dated for a brief time between 2011 to 2012.

Flo Rida confirmed the relationship during an appearance on The Wendy Williams Show.

At the time he revealed via WRNB: “I’ve always been a fan of her doing her modeling thing. She’s a beautiful individual. Meeting her I realized she had a beautiful heart.

"Initially it was seeing her out and about while I was working and she was working.

'So we got each other’s contact, I pursued and we kept talking to each other. Right now we are talking and I’m definitely feeling her and vice versa.”

Eva has one daughter from her previous relationship with Kevin McCall and two sons from her marriage to Michael Sterling.

Melyssa Ford

Melyssa Ford is a Canadian media personality and actress.

She dated Flo Rida between 2011 to 2012.

Following their split, she opened up about their relationship and revealed that they were "doomed" because they had nothing in common, according to YBF.

How many kids does Flo Rida have?

In September 2016, Flo Rida welcomed his son Zohar Paxton with Alexis Adams.

The pair previously met in 2015, but at this time, it is unclear if they were in a relationship.

In May 2018, Alexis sat down with Daily Mail and explained that Flo Rida was not a part of their life.

She further explained that at first, he denied being the child's father but a DNA test later revealed a 99.9 percent match.

"Flo Rida has only seen his son once during the paternity test but kept his shades on and his back turned," Alexis told the outlet.
