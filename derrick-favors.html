Childhood and Education

Derrick Bernard Favors, a.k.a Derrick Favors, was born on July 15, 1991, in Atlanta, Georgia. Derrick was born to proud parents Johnnie Parker (Father) and Deandra Favors (mother).

Derrick’s Father and mother fell in love while working in Atlanta for Eastern Airlines. First, Tiffany (Derrick’s elder sister) was born, then Derrick, and after him, Brandon (Derrick’s younger brother) was born.

Everything was going fine until Derrick’s Father left the family. After his dad was gone, the family’s expenses came on Deandra’s shoulders. Derrick didn’t even know who his Father was until he was eighteen.

However, even after being a single mom, she didn’t let her children give up on their dreams. Instead, she raised her children like a lioness.

Besides, Derrick went to South Atlanta High School, where he was part of the high school basketball team and was ranked one of the best high school players in 2009.

He started getting good responses and was appreciated by everyone, which further helped him in his professional career.
