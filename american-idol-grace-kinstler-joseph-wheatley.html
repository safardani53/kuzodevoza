Who is American Idol star Grace Kinstler's boyfriend?

Grace Kinstler is dating musician Joseph Wheatley, a graduate of Northeastern University who is currently living in Texas.

Grace and Joseph were in a long-distance relationship during her time on American Idol.

He remained supportive of his girlfriend, posting pictures of her on Instagram while promoting her talents.
