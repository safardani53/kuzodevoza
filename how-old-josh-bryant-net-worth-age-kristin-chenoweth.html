Who is Josh Bryant?

Josh was born in Arkansas and is currently a resident of Nashville. His father is a farmer, and Josh has two older sisters.

He is a guitar player, and in 2012, he formed a country-rock band with his friends called Backroad Anthem. The band has opened up for several artists, including Josh Turner and Chris Young.

Outside of Backroad Anthem, Josh works as a guitarist for two other artists and has also performed with Ty Herndon.

Josh's Instagram bio also states that he is a "Lover of Jesus."
