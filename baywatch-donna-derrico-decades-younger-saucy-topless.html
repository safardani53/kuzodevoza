BAYWATCH star Donna D'Errico looks red hot as she strips off for charity.

The 51-year-old actress was defying the decades as she covered herself in scarlet paint, in pictures released on Wednesday.

The exceptional beauty - who played Donna Marco in the TV series, alongside Pamela Anderson - posed up a storm in the pictures for @PaintedPrincessProject - which are reminiscent of her famous red swimsuit pose.

The profits from the prints go to A21.org to help rescue victims of human trafficking around the world, educate the vulnerable, and restore survivors to an independent life.

D'Errico managed to get some of the paint in her honey-blonde hair, which was styled in voluminous waves around her shoulders.

She's changed her hair style since her Baywatch days, when she sported bangs, and looked remarkably fresh faced in the images.

D'Errico also has a son, Rhyan, born in 1993.

Besides Baywatch, D’Errico’s other credits include Comedy Central’s Reno 911! and the movie Austin Powers in Goldmember.

She most recently guest-starred in Brooklyn Nine-Nine.
