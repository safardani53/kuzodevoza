A WOMAN has revealed the five perfumes she can’t stand - so if you want to smell fresh, you’ll need to listen up.

Bailee, a perfume lover and beauty fan, has a large collection of designer scents, and has shared the sprays that make her gag and leave her nostrils feeling burnt.

Not only this, but she described a popular scent as smelling like body odour in a bottle, so prepare to be attacked.

Sharing her thoughts on social media, the woman who is known online as ‘Little Miss Perfume’, said: “Here are a couple of perfumes I would never recommend.

“They burn my nostrils and they make me wanna run away.

“First one - Angel by Mugler.

“This is so pungent and gets stuck in my throat and I kind of wanna gag.”

Bailee then shared her thoughts on the Maison Louis Marie Antridirs Cassis scent, as she added: “So bad.

“Minty, Vicks, bug spray.

“I just sprayed this on my arm and I can't wait to get it off.”

Prepare to be attacked if Gucci is your perfume of choice, as the brunette beauty continued: 

“I feel like i'm gonna make a lot of people mad at this one, but any of the Gucci Bloom - particularly the yellow one.

“Just grass. It smells like grass to me.”

Following this, Bailee shared her honest thoughts on an Akro perfume, as well as a popular Tom Ford option.

She concluded: “Next one is Night by Akro.

“These are all based off of people’s secret vices - this one being like a hook up, one night stand.

“Straight up BO [body odour] in a bottle. It smells like sweaty bodies, so strange.

“Lastly - Tom Ford Bitter Peach.

“Bitter is correct. I mean I get it, but I also kind of don't.” 

The TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @littlemissperfume, has clearly left many open-mouthed, as it has quickly amassed over 7,000 likes.

But social media users were left divided at Bailee’s claims - while some were eager to disagree, others couldn’t agree more. 

One person said: “Gucci Bloom legit smells like heaven to me.” 

Another added: “I love Angel.”

A third commented: “Bitter peach didn't deserve this.”

However at the same time, many others were eager to agree with Bailee. 

One beauty fan wrote: “Everytime I smell Gucci Bloom I get a migraine within like 10 mins!! I hate it.”

A second chimed in: “Agree with all of these!!!!”
