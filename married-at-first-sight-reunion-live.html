    Executive producer of the show Chris Coelen revealed in an interview with People how some of the applicants are chosen.

    He said: “The men and the women are treated exactly the same. We go to bars, mixers, singles events, and church groups… We also go on every dating site you could possibly think of – OkCupid, Match.com, Tinder, Hinge.”

    Coelen added that the producers go on Facebook and talk to family and friends to garner as many people as possible for the audition process.
