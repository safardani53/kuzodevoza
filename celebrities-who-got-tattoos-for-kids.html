Kristin Cavallari

The Very Cavallari star showed off two new designs she got courtesy of celebrity tattoo artist JonBoy.

The mom-of-three debuted the results on her Instagram Stories, including a forearm tattoo with the script initials of her children: Camden, Jaxon and Saylor.

Cavallari also got another meaningful design: a small butterfly on her wrist.

"Butterflies have been such a sign for me when going through difficult times," she explained alongside a close-up of the design. The new tattoos came four months after the reality star announced her divorce from Jay Cutler.

Kylie Jenner

The Kylie Cosmetics mogul showed off what appeared to be the the time "4:43" tattooed on her inner forearm in July 2020. Days after Stormi's birth on February 1, 2018, TMZ reported 4:43 was when Jenner's daughter entered the world after the outlet obtained a copy of her birth certificate.

While she kicked back and filmed a selfie video with her makeup artist Ariel Tejada, Jenner flashed the tiny tattoo on her Instagram Story, which was first spotted by Page Six.

It wasn't long before fans pointed out that Flynn's name was misspelled as 'Frynn.' Whoopsie!
