Queen Queen Sonja of Norway was born on July 4, 1937 in Oslo, Norway (She's 86 years old now).

She is best known as the wife of King King Harald V of Norway and as the Queen consort of Norway, a title she assumed in January 1991 after the death of King Olav V. In 52 years, she became Norway's first Queen consort.

She attended the École Professionnelle des Jeunes Filles in Switzerland, where she studied accounting, social science, and fashion design. She went on to the University of Oslo to get her bachelor's degree in French, English, and art history.
