Chris Cuomo is a well-known television journalist who works for CNN. Cuomo Prime Time is a show he hosts. Cuomo Prime Time is a regular weeknight CNN show, and he has a slew of fans all over the world.

Salary and Net Worth

CNN pays him a whopping four dollars per hour, making him one of the highest-paid journalists in the world. His earnings always increase his net worth. Chris Cuomo has a net worth of $9 million as of August 2023.

Awards

Throughout his career, he has been nominated for several Emmy Awards. He won an Emmy for his Good Morning America profile of a 12-year-old poet named Mattie Stepanek. He is one of the youngest people ever to win an Emmy.

Wife, Married

He is married to his wife Christina Greeven Cuomo, and the couple married in 2001. The couple has three children: Bella Cuomo, Mario Cuomo, and Carolina Regina Cuomo. Education, Childhood, and Parents

He received his education at The Albany Academy. He then transferred to Yale University, where he earned his undergraduate degree. Fordham University provided him with his J.D. (Juris Doctor). He is also a practicing attorney.

His father’s name is Mario Cuomo. He was a Democratic Party politician. He served as New York’s 52nd Governor. From 1979 to 1982, he was also the Lieutenant Governor of New York.

From 1975 to 1978, he was also the Secretary of State of New York. Matilda Cuomo is his mother’s name. She was a proponent of women’s and children’s rights, as well as the former First Lady of New York from 1983 to 1994.
