Fuzzy-faced fanatic with beard crafted to look like a dog pipped at the post for World Beard and Mou

 Posted on April 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  139 words  |  Aldo Pusey

MANE man Isaiah Webb was bushed into second place for his ast-hounding wolf-like facial hair at the 2017 World Beard and Moustache Championships.

He was beaten Michael Wollin whose 60cm locks won him best full beard at the show in Austin, Texas.

Jason Kiley’s intimately coiled coiffure saw him crowned best in show beating punk John Banks’s curls by a whisker.

The bi-annual event features 26 bizarre categories from four main categories for best moustache, partial beard, full beard and, for the first time, ladies’ beard.

Over a thousand flocked to the three-day tournament, which was the biggest since the modern version of the age-old tradition started in Germany in 1990.

Competitors spend hours before the big day, brushing and blow-drying their beards before using extra strong hair products to fix them in place.
