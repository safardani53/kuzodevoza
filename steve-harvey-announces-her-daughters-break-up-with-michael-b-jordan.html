Steve Harvey Announces Her Daughters Break Up With Michael B. Jordan

 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  575 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Steve Harvey sends his best wishes to Michael B. Jordan after his divorce from Lori Harvey. Lori’s father disclosed her split from Creed actor on The Steve Harvey Morning Show on June 6. When asked how he felt about the split, Steve said,

“I heard about it, and I wish them the best.” Lori has my full support. She’s my child. I adore her. “I am behind her.”

Lori, 25, and Jordan, 35, had a tough time dating in public, according to Judge Steve Harvey, but their relationship ended peacefully. Steve also said that he still has romantic sentiments for the Black Panther star.

“From what I know, he’s still a nice man.” It’s a divorce. People split up all the time, so I’m sure they’ll be alright.”

A source close to the former couple announced their breakup after a year and a half of dating.

Lori and Michael B Jordan’s breakup has been confirmed by Steve Harvey.

On a June 6 broadcast of The Steve Harvey Morning Show, Steve Harvey addressed Lori Harvey and Michael B. Jordan’s separation, first delivering a somber perspective on their love journey coming to an end, then making a few jokes about it. The following is what Steve Harvey had to say about the former:

I still have to go to work… I still have to look after my family. Lori has my full support since she is my daughter. I adore her and support her.”

Steve went on:

“It’s difficult to be young and in the spotlight while also having a great relationship.” Look, as long as everyone can go in peace and remain friends… Nobody said they shattered any windows or anything.”

However, Steve Harvey described Jordan as a “very respectable” man in early May, and he showed his support for the Black Panther star and his daughter. Despite the fact that they allegedly “still admire one other,” their romantic connection did not work out.

More about Michael B. Jordan and Lori Harvey’s relationship

Michael B. Jordan, 35, and Lori Harvey, 25, the creator of SKN, met years ago via mutual acquaintances. They originally started dating in November 2020 and joined Instagram in January 2021. Jordan, who is typically quiet, even talked frankly about the affair in a December interview and made plenty of room on his Instagram grid for Harvey throughout the relationship. Meanwhile, it seems that she has removed his presence from her page.

Lori and Jordan’s divorce was made public over the weekend. According to a source,

“Michael and Lori are both broken-hearted. They are still in love.”

Lori and Jordan’s first anniversary was in November 2021, and Jordan surprised his then-girlfriend with a caviar-filled meal. Steve raved over his daughter’s relationship in March 2021, saying:

“I tried hard not to like him. I wanted to discover something wrong with him so I could dig in… since I did get rid of all of them.”

According to a source, Lori “realized they weren’t on the same page” as she and the Black Panther star “were making plans for their future.” Lori and Jordan’s agents, meanwhile, have reluctant to comment on their split. Lori has a history of dating celebs. She has previously been associated with Lewis Hamilton, Trey Songz, Future, Sean “Diddy” Combs, and his son Justin Dior Combs. Michael B. Jordan has previously been connected to Cindy Bruna, Kiki Layne, and YouTuber Catherine Paiz. He was also connected to Black Panther co-star Lupita Nyong’o, however, both stated that they were only pals.
