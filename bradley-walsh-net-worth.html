Who is Bradley Walsh?

Bradley Walsh, was born in Watford, Hertfordshire, on June 4, 1960.

He is an English actor, TV presenter, comedian, singer, and former professional footballer.

A man of many talents, as a teenager Bradley enjoyed a career on the football pitch.

He played for Brentford in their reserve team, with stints on loan at Dunstable Town, Barnet, Tring Town, Boreham Wood and Chalfont St Peter.

However, his footballing success only lasted for a few years before ankle injuries forced him out of the sport at just 22.

But, as one door closed, another opened - and Bradley walked straight into the world of showbiz.

In addition to his television career, Walsh often appears in theatre shows, particularly pantomimes.

And if his CV wasn't impressive enough, Walsh also has a background in singing.

The TV star has released two albums - the first was Chasing Dreams, which peaked at 10 on the UK Albums Chart and the second was titled When You're Smiling, which also hit the charts at number 11.

Donna Derby has been married to Bradley Walsh since 1997.
