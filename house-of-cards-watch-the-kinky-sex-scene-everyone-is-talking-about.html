‘House Of Cards’ Sex Scene — Season 2’s Kinky Threesome Everyone’s Talking About

We knew from the moment we saw Claire and Edward laughing and flirting that something sexy was going to happen between them — we just weren’t expecting Frank to join in on the fun!

When the president walks in on his first lady and his bodyguard enjoying a few drinks, the sexual tension is palpable. Edward quickly jumps up, appearing guilty, and Claire explains that Edward had cut his hand on a broken glass and she had to dress his wound.

But then Frank takes over doctor duty, tenderly cradling Edward’s hand is his own as he tends to his bodyguard’s injury. There’s that sexual tension again!

‘House Of Cards’ Sex Scene — Edward & Frank Share A Kiss

Edward technically makes the first move when he starts caressing Frank’s hand. Then the three exchange some very meaningful glances before Claire leans in to kiss Edward’s hand. Before we knew it, Claire was kissing Frank, Edward was kissing Claire’s neck and then Frank and Edward were kissing each other — wow!

We knew Frank experimented with a male classmate in college, but this is one plot twist we weren’t expecting. What happens next is left up to the imagination, but the threesome clearly … well, had a threesome!

The best part of the clip is when Claire and Frank see each other in the kitchen the next morning.

Frank casually reveals that he “slept like a baby,” to which Claire responds, “Good. You needed that.” Ha!
