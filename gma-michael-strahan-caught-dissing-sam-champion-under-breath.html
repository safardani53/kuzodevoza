MICHAEL Strahan has been caught on camera poking fun at his Good Morning America co-star Sam Champion.

The awkward moment happened live on air.

Michael, 51, and Sam, 62, stood outside the GMA studio as they prepared for NFL star Rob Gronkowski and his girlfriend to do the Ice Bath Challenge.

As the two hosts walked up to the platform, they looked for Gronk and Camile, but couldn't find them.

Michael and Sam turned to each other and asked at the same time: "Do you want to get in?"

"No, I'm not gonna get in," Michael said while wearing a pristine gray suit.

SEEING DOUBLE

Aside from working at GMA, Sam recently took on a new role.

He is the new co-anchor of Eyewitness News Mornings @ 10.

Sam was recently stumped on a live broadcast while losing focus.

The meteorologist shared a video from the morning show to his Instagram account.

Sam introduced Dr. Desmond Sutton and read out his credentials while the medical expert joined the ABC anchors via video chat.

Immediately after acknowledging Dr Desmond, Sam and his co-stars, Mike Marza and Shirleen Allicot, appeared to have an epiphany.

"We just all noticed at the same time that you look very familiar to us," Sam said, speaking for him and the on-air crew.

"Do you, sir, have a twin?"

Dr. Desmond told the anchors that he does.

Sam then asked him a follow-up question.

"Does your twin work for ABC News?" he wondered.

SAM'S SURPRISE

Dr. Desmond admitted that his twin brother does, in fact, work for ABC.

This led to Sam's co-host, Shirleen revealing that ABC medical correspondent Dr. Darien Sutton was Dr. Desmond's identical twin.

"WATCH a true and honest SURPRISE [unfold] on this morning’s 10 AM show!!!!!," Sam captioned the video.
