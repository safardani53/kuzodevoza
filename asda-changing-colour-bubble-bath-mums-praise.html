Mums praise Asdas 1.20 colour-changing bubble bath for ending bathtime tantrums The Sun

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  583 words  |  Martina Birk

WHILE some parents are fortunate enough to have children who LOVE taking a bath, others aren't quite as lucky.

That said, ASDA's funky new colour-changing bubble bath might be about to put an end to bathroom tantrums for good - and the best part is, it only costs £1.20.

Out of the water, the bubble bath is orange, but once added to the tub it turns a beautiful bright blue.

Needless to say, parents say their kids love the "magical" orange-scented product - and one mum also added a video to the post to show how it works.

Sharing it on the Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, she wrote:"ASDA magic bubble bath £1.27 smells amazing too!"

Another mum tagged her kids' gran and wrote: "Next time you go to ASDA I know one of your granddaughters would love it!"

While one commented: "I bought this and can confirm it smells great and the kids love it.

"I put it in the paddling pool in the summer."

Others agreed, explaining they had tried the product and had very enthusiastic responses from their kids.

But it's not just little ones who love the bubble bath, with adults also taking to the comments, with one writing: "I NEED this in my life!"

It's not the only treat ASDA is selling for kids at the moment, with the supermarket having launched toy trolleys and tills, too.

They're also selling an adorable pair of Frozen heels that light up when tapped against the ground.

The £14 lilac pumps feature a one-inch crystal heel and a shimmering, iridescent flower embellishment - and they're flying off the shelves online.
