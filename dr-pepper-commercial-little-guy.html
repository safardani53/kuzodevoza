Who is the little guy in the Dr. Pepper commercial?

 Posted on May 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  365 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

LIL' SWEET is a fictional miniature musician who finds his home in various Dr. Pepper advertisements.

The beloved character has appeared in commercials since 2015.

Who is the little guy in the Dr. Pepper commercial?

Lil' Sweet is seemingly the mascot for Diet Dr. Pepper but sometimes shows in ads for the regular version of the soda.

Multitalented entertainer Justin Guarini portrays him.

Born on October 28, 1978, Guarini is a native of Georgia.

He began singing with the Atlanta Boy Choir at the age of four.

Guarini performed on stage throughout high school and college and learned to play the piano, guitar, and banjo.

He studied vocal performance and theatre in Philadelphia and New York before auditioning for American Idol in 2002.

Did Justin Guarini win American Idol?

Justin Guarini became the first runner-up on American Idol, ultimately losing to Kelly Clarkson during season one of the singing competition.

Shortly after he was accepted on the show, Guarini was offered a role on Broadway - he chose to stick with Idol.

He went on to sign with 19 Management, landing a record deal with RCA.

Guarini finally made his Broadway debut in 2010, appearing in Women on the Verge of a Nervous Breakdown.
