TEEN Mom Ashley Jones has slammed her co-stars and thrown shade at her enemy Briana DeJesus via a Tweet after their recent physical brawl.

In a new interview, Kiaya, 22, and other Family Reunion stars commented on the chaotic fight caught on camera- and Ashley, 25, was not happy.

Sitting on a virtual panel with Teen Mom stars Jade Cline, Maci Bookout, and Catelynn Lowell, Kiaya kicked off: "I can't get offended about what this girl is saying about me because obviously, that whole family's dynamic is chaos."

Kiaya continued: "It's always something - they're comfortable in that chaos.

"What makes me think I'm going to be better than them is that you're not going to try and be chaotic with me too. You know?"

The mediator on the panel asked directly about Ashley's confrontation with Briana featured in the trailer.

Catelynn concluded: "If someone gets spit on, all your morals and ethics go out the window."

"It's giving main character and I'm only on two to three episodes."

In another Tweet, she announced: "Now that my brother is gone, I'm really ready to eat y'all hoes up, so please don't say another word."

Ashley shockingly lost her brother, Deandre, last week and his unexpected death is still under investigation.

She concluded her frustrated internet rant by tweeting: "By the way, merry f***ing Christmas."

SHOCKING SCENE

The shade thrown follows Ashley and Briana's explosive physical altercation while filming Teen Mom: Family Reunion.

A preview clip for Family Reunion season two was released earlier this month after the reality stars headed on a retreat to wrap up the year.

Tensions ran high as the girls' moms were invited along for an energy-filled getaway complete with cabin parties, bungee jumping and white water rafting.

In one snippet of the trailer, Ashley screamed "f**k you" at her nemesis Briana from across the room.

The comment resulted in Briana lunging into the air to attack her younger co-star.

Security and production jumped in to hold Briana back.

The wild moment left both cast and crew in shock as the nasty feud has continued between the two Teen Moms.

TENSE MOMENT

Briana and Ashley have not made up in recent months after the fight.

Briana's uneasiness around Ashley was obvious when she stormed off the stage during the first-ever Teen Mom: Next Chapter reunion, which was filmed after the Family Reunion spinoff.

In a clip shared on the show's official Instagram, the entire cast prepared to reflect on the season while onstage with their host Dr. Drew.

The girls were getting hair and makeup touch-ups when a production assistant approached the stage.

"Ashley's gonna sit down, and we're gonna be all good," he told the cast.

"I don't think any of us were gonna do anything anyways," Amber Portwood replied.

Briana didn't agree and chimed in: "I feel like this setup is not what I wanted to because I don't feel comfortable."

The MTV star then ripped her microphone off and stormed off set in an emotional moment.

The mother of two was then filmed standing in the green room talking to production.

"I just thought I could do it, but no I can't," she admitted, wiping her tears with a tissue.

"I can't. It's just major anxiety, and this is my first time seeing her and I don't think I can keep my composure."

Jade then entered the room to check on her friend, asking: "Are you okay?"

"I just can't be next to her," Briana replied, adding: "I can't do it."
