15 Celebrity Christmas Cards That Remind Us 2020 Wasn't All Bad

 Posted on February 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  767 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Here is a roundup of some of the best celebrity Christmas and holiday cards of 2020 — including Kelly Ripa, Taylor Swift, and Victoria Beckham.

By Anna Quintana

Dec. 25 2020, Published 3:00 p.m. ET

Let's be honest. Christmas just does not feel the same in 2020.

However, traditions are meant to be passed down and, despite the awful year, 2020 holiday cards definitely brought some cheer. 

Article continues below advertisement

Luckily for us, some of our favorite celebrities shared their Christmas cards as a reminder that there is still a lot to be thankful for. 

From Taylor Swift's cat-centered card to TikTok star Tabitha Brown's pajama-clad family pic, these celebrity Christmas cards hit the mark and give us a bit of hope for 2021. 

Tabitha Brown

2020 was definitely Tabitha Brown's year. The actress became a bonafide TikTok star thanks to her vegan-friendly recipes and happy-go-lucky personality.

So, it was no surprise that Tabitha's Christmas card features her three kids and her hubby (and dog!) in matching Christmas PJs.

Of course, Taylor's three cats — Meredith, Olivia, and Benjamin— would be the stars of her Christmas card.

David and Victoria Beckham

Amanda Kloots
