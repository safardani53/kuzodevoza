Who is El Debarge:

El Debarge is a famous R&B Singer. He was born on June 4, 1961 and his birthplace is Detroit, MI. El is also well known as, Lead singer of the family R&B group DeBarge, known for singles like “Rhythm of the Night” and “All This Love.” He has been featured on singles for other artists, like “After the Dance” by Fourplay.

El is originated from United States. He has collaborated with Quincy Jones.
