Fast &amp; Furious Live sneak peek ahead of UK tour next year with high octane stunts and the movie's be

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  381 words  |  Martina Birk

THESE incredible photos give fans a first glimpse at the all-new Fast & Furious live action show.

The £25million high-octane project will tour arenas across the UK next year - starting at London's O2 on January 19.

The stadium event will feature the best stunts from the eight F&F films and some of the finest supercars to grace the franchise.

While Dwayne Johnson and Vin Diesel won't be around there's a whole new storyline and some incredible 3D trickery to transport you around the globe.

Fans who go to see the show at Liverpool Arena, Belfast SSE Arena, Arena Birmingham, Glasgow SSE Hydro, Manchester Arena, Newcastle Metro Radio Arena and Sheffield FlyDSA Arena will all get a taste of LA or Rio.

Eleven stunt drivers are signed up to the show with former Top Gear man Rowland French helping to write and direct.

New stars Elysia Wren and Mark Ebulué will lead the show but the real stars will be the cars.

There are the Japanese drift legends like the Nissan Skyline, supercars like the Lamborghini Murcielago and the Radical Flip Car driven by Luke Evans in Fast & Furious 6.

Think of it as Cirque du Soleil for petrolheads.
