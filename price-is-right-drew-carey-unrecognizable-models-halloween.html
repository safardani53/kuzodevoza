LONGTIME TV host Drew Carey and The Price Is Right models have gone all-out for Tuesday's Halloween episode.

Drew, 65, channeled his inner Hulk Hogan for the WWE-themed broadcast.

Drew walked out to explosive pyrotechnics in the showstopping look on October 31. 

He donned huge blond 80s hair, a full muscle suit (which showed one of his pseudo nipples), and a championship belt.

As he walked out in a wrestler-style entrance, he broke a fake microphone in half, yelling.

The entire Price is Right episode was treated like a WWE match.

And Alexis Gaube also followed suit with a bikini WWE look while they all presented the various prizes.

At one point, the announcer referenced the late Bob Barker and warned: "Get your pet spayed and neutered whether they like it or not!"

DREW'S WINNING TRANSFORMATION

Season 52 of The Price Is Right is the first season at a new studio.

It's also the first since the death of longtime host Bob Barker - premiering on September 25.

The Price Is Right left Television City after 50 years for a new location in nearby Glendale, L.A.

Fans noticed a bigger change as Drew walked out on stage for the premiere, flashing a smile in a chic suit.

The 65-year-old ditched his facial hair and seemingly shed a few pounds, making for an extremely fresh look.

LOSS OF A LEGEND

Bob died on the morning of Saturday, August 26, 2023, in his Los Angeles home - at the time it was said to be due to natural causes.

He was 99 at the time of his death.

Bob began hosting The Price is Right in 1972 and held an impressive 35-year run before his retirement.

Due to his passion for animal rights, his iconic tagline at the end of each episode was: “Help control the pet population.

"Have your pet spayed or neutered."

The iconic television star's achievements include 19 Emmys, a Guinness World Records award, and being inducted into the Television Academy Hall of Fame.
