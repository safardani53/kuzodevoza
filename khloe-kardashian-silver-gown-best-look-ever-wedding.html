KHLOE Kardashian has stunned fans as she donned a silver gown while attending a friend's wedding.

Fans of the third eldest Kardashian sister flooded the star's Instagram with love for her look.

Photographed by Ricardo Horatio Nelson, Khloe, 39, posted two photos on Thursday to show her outfit.

The first snap posted by the Good American owner was a close-up shot of herself.

Khloe wore a fitted silver metallic gown that was low-cut and strapless.

On the star's chest, fans saw a big diamond necklace that hung around her neck.

MEDICAL SCARE

The Instagram post came after Khloe updated followers on a medical procedure she underwent after a skin cancer scare.

The Hulu star donned a bandage on her face for several months, speaking at length about her condition on The Kardashians.

She posted a close-up photo of her face online, revealing that a large chunk of her cheek is missing after being removed for testing.

In the photo, the mom-of-two appeared to be posing in a mirror, partially obscuring her face with her phone.

The outline of her cheek was visible, however, and she drew an arrow pointing toward it that further highlighted the alteration to her appearance.

Khloe captioned her post: "Not that we needed the arrow to point out the huge indentation on the side of my face.... but because we had to remove the tumor from my cheek I was left with an indentation."
