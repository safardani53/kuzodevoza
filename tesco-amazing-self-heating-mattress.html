However, there are some brilliant products you can buy to help combat the cold. 

Shoppers at Tesco are rushing to buy a self-heating mattress topper that promises to help you stay snug in the cold. 

The Silent Night Heat Genie Mattress Topper currently costs just £20 with a Tesco Clubcard. 

It was spotted on the Money Saver By Dansway Facebook page, showing the product in store.

The toppers come in single, double and king size meaning you can buy exactly what fit you need for your bed.

“The foil lining reflects body heat back to the sleeper and retains warmth, keeping your bed cosy and warm without the need for electricity.”
