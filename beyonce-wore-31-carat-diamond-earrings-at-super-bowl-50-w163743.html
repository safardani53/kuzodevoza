Beyonce Wore 31-Carat Diamond Earrings at Super Bowl 50

 Posted on March 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  262 words  |  Martina Birk

You can’t be a Queen Bey without a few crown jewels! Beyoncé stormed the field during the Super Bowl 50 Halftime Show on Sunday, February 7, in Santa Clara, California, wearing massive 31-carat, football-shaped diamond earrings.

PHOTOS: Beyoncé’s Best Instagram Styles

And the bling didn’t stop there! The “Formation” singer, 34, not only donned Lorraine Schwartz’s eye-popping ear candy, but the designer’s 30-carat bezel ring, 26-carat champagne diamond ring and two diamond wraparound pinkie rings. (FYI: Her nails were polished with OPI’s Black Onyx.) For those counting at home, that’s more than 100 carats of diamond baubles on Bey!

While an insider tells Us that she had more than 15 outfits to choose from, Beyoncé, whose 4-year-old daughter, Blue Ivy, watched from the stands, ultimately chose a custom leather DSquared2 jacket with a gold harness inspired by a late great. “Beyoncé wanted to pay homage to Michael,” the singer’s stylist, Marni Senofonte, told Essence. “She has always said he is her biggest music influence.”
