KYLIE Jenner has shown off a brand new limited-edition $2.5k Louis Vuitton purse amid backlash over flaunting her wealth.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum has been criticized for years for brandishing her wealth on social media.

But that hasn't stopped Kylie, 25, from flaunting her new limited edition $2.5k Louis Vuitton bag she was gifted by the luxury brand.

The reality star took to her Instagram Stories on Wednesday night to snap a photo of the special edition mini purse designed by Yayoi Kusama.

The small bag was painted with pumpkins in different shades of blue.

Kylie captioned her post: "Thank you @louisvuitton!! @yayoikusama."

IN THE BAG

Kylie has received rampant backlash from fans who believe she unjustly flaunts her wealth whenever possible.

Last month, the mother of two boasted about owning a $3,200 light-up bag in a new video despite her follower's complaints.

The reality star took to her Instagram Stories to show off the designer accessory.

Kylie focused the camera on the Loewe LED Puzzle Bag from the FW22 Menswear Collection in the clip.

She held the bag with one hand while walking down a hallway, gushing over the fashion item in her caption.

"This bag makes me happy," the beauty mogul wrote.

The tiny purse that lights up along the sides has a price tag of $3,255.

The post was a bold move for the 25-year-old, who's been slammed numerous times for showing off her earnings and other expensive items.

OVER-THE-TOP CLOSET

In September, Kylie gave her followers a glimpse inside her walk-in closet, jam-packed with hundreds of designer shoes and handbags.

The Hulu star posed in front of an illuminated glass shelf, which featured several costly heels, purses, and clutch bags - from a white Chanel purse to Hermes Birkin bags.
