How much are Christmas bedding sets?

Christmas bedding sets are usually the same price as standard bedding sets, but it all depends on where you're buying from, and the size of duvet cover you're buying.

Single duvet covers are always cheaper and the best value on our list is the Christmas Dogs Reversible Set from Asda George, priced at £10. The most expensive single cover in our edit is the Next 3D Pom Pom Christmas Tree Set, priced at £42.

For super king duvets, it's usual for prices to start around £60 for the cheapest and climb up to £150 for the very best fabrics and brands.

Where to buy Christmas bedding sets?

The market for Christmas bedding sets is gaining momentum all the time and there are a huge number of retailers picking up on the trend.

If you're shopping online, the choice for where to buy is huge. Some of the best retailers we found in our search include Wayfair, Dunelm, Matalan, Amazon, M&S, Asda and The Range, to name but a few.

What to look for in a Christmas bedding set

A striking festive design has to be at the top of the must-have list for any Christmas bedding, surely?

Consider what the bedding set is made from — is it polyester, polycotton or 100% cotton? Also, double check it's available in your size as some festive bedding sets are limited to just one or two sizes.

Check what closure the duvet has — most sets are either zipped, buttoned or poppered on.
